Dear Artist,

Welcome behind the scenes at UNHEARD! We are thrilled that we have sparked your interest and would be delighted to welcome you to one of our upcoming shows!

Why UNHEARD?

There is a lot of good music being created all around us—but often it goes unheard! In the age of playlist algorithms and follower counts, it is no longer just the quality of the music that determines the attention it receives. We want to counteract this with UNHEARD, because we believe that good music is independent of its reach!

How does it work?

For each show, we invite four artists to present their music. If you want to participate, send us two of your songs. Ideally, you should also send us a short voice message introducing yourself and your tracks and telling us what you've always wanted to say on the radio. The show is broadcast every third Tuesday of the month on Colaboradio at “Freier Rundfunk - Berlin Brandenburg” (fr-bb.org/). Afterwards, we upload the show to our archive on Soundcloud.

Postproduction

We use our social media channels to promote our shows and featured guests. To do this, we need a picture of you that will be featured on the cover of the corresponding show. We usually link the posts about a show to your profiles. If you do not want this, please write this in the text field below.

Have Mercy!

We do Radio Moafunk and UNHEARD out of love for music. We don't get paid for it and do all the production in our spare time. So, every now and then, we might forget a social media post or a recording might have its flaws. Please bear with us!
